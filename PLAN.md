# South India Crop Market Website Plan

## Goal
Help a farmer in South India decide what crop to plant next using commercial value, demand, and price trends.

## MVP Features (implemented now)
- Farmer profile input: state, season, soil type, water availability, investment level, risk appetite, and farm size.
- Crop recommendation engine: ranks crops by suitability + commercial score.
- Commercial analysis table: yield, farm-gate price, cost, margin, ROI, and break-even yield per acre.
- Price comparison dashboard: side-by-side farm-gate price vs mandi price, 30-day trend, and spread.
- Quick insights: best priced crop, strongest trend crop, and most stable crop.

## Phase 2 Features
- District-level mandi integration (Agmarknet/API ingestion).
- Weather forecast + rainfall risk and sowing-window alerts.
- Fertilizer/seed cost tracker per crop and per district.
- Historical seasonality charts (3-5 years) for prices and arrivals.
- WhatsApp/SMS alerts when selected crop price crosses threshold.

## Phase 3 Features
- Personalized planting portfolio (split acreage across crops).
- Contract farming / export opportunity matching.
- Multi-language UI (Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam).
- Yield prediction from satellite/weather signals.
